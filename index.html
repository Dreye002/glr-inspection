Thorough & Documented

What We Check

A real inspection looks at the whole roof system, not just the surface.

  • The roof surface, shingles or tile, for wear, damage, and aging
  • Flashing around chimneys, vents, and walls, a common leak point
  • Roof penetrations and seals
  • Valleys, where water concentrates and leaks often start
  • Gutters and drainage
  • Visible leak areas and water staining
  • Attic access where available and permitted, to check from underneath
  • Photo documentation of everything we find

Local Roof Issues We See

Every San Gabriel Valley city has its own roofing challenges.

Pasadena & Altadena
